## El primero

On a sweltering day, the keys are pressed and a mouse is clicked.  "El primero..."  

A simple blog is created and the first post of the week is typed up.  Hopefully, more posts will come.  I'm gonna be honest: I have no idea if I'm writing this for myself or for others.  It's mostly for myself because I haven't even shared this with anyone.  The odds of someone stumbling upon this by chance are low.  My writing voice will change depending the mood and the audience I imagine at the time of writing. 

DO NOT expect consistency with the POV nor writing style.  If I wanted to retain readers, I guess I'd probably try to establish a formal tone.  But this is just a baby blorg with 1.5 readers, including myself. 

I'll try my best to keep the poetry and microfiction as separate posts for organization's sake... Chau for now?
