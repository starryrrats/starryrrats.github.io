---
title: The Steeple
layout: post
tags: [microfiction, "tales of delusion"]
---
The houses aren't tall enough.  My apartment building isn't tall enough.  If I want it, I must seek the steepled place where what I want to achieve is an abomination.  Mother would watch me-- wait, no, there will not be a single person observing me on the special, unholy day.  Even though I wasn't born in the absence of humans, I shall seek a time when there is a most prominent absence.  Then I'll celebrate my last achievement, and the stone grips easily in my palms.  I've reached the artificial peak, and from here on, nothing will be as high nor euphoric, and everything becomes a blur as the wind strengthens its whistle.  Things are looking up, despite the final destination.
