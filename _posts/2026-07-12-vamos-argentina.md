Let's fucking goooooo!  I spent my evening hunting for streams to watch Argentina vs. Switzerland, FIFA WC 2026.  The live chats are certainly something... Many, many slurs.  I missed Argentina's first goal, but I saw the Swiss score.  My boyfriend was worried that they'd score again but I was optimistic about Argentina making another goal.  Embolo got red carded shortly after, as well.  He was given a yellow card for a simulation, and it was his second yellow card so that became a red card, and he was sent off the field.  Also, I learned that a simulation is faking, basically.  Embolo threw himself down without receiving any prior contact, hence the card.  I'm new to football, okay?

It took a looong time for Argentina to get a lead again.  I didn't see Argentina's first goal in the 10th minute, and it wasn't until minute 112, that they scored again.  Then they made another goal pretty quickly :0, in minute 120.  Before the second goal, I wondered what happened if the timer ended with the score as 1-1.  My boyfriend said they go on to do penalties and that games can't end in ties.  So I've learned a few things about football today.  Maybe this is the start of my journey to become un futbolero, hmhm...  Their next match is against England.  I really hope they wiiiin.  Argentina has had a 12 match win streak for this year's WC, so far.  Keep it up!

It was great, feeling the nostalgia upon finding the game again.  I think tomorrow I'll look into Omega Strikers.  It's a free game, 3v3, with the same or similar concept to soccer, I think, and it's been in my library forever.
