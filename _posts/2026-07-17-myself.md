If we were in another life, <br>
We'd be twins, twice the strife.<br>
I'd act as I do now;<br>
I'd boss you around.

I'd have to be your parent.<br>
Remind you of rent,<br>
Clean up your mess,<br>
Oh you'd still give me stress.<br>

But I'd love you all the same,<br>
I'd look at you with disdain,<br>
Yet soften when you cry.<br>
I'd hold you and on the ground, we'd lie.

As I do now, in this other life,<br>
I'd do my best to distract you from the fights.<br>
We'd face everything together.<br>
Separation? Why, simply never.

I hate you and despise you,<br>
But we'd still be bound true,<br>
And for every meltdown of yours<br>
I'd make you tea and set it on the floor.
