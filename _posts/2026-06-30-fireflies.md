I’m sitting at a park I used to visit when I lived closer to it.  The park’s not too far from my workplace, but I’ve only visited it when I decide to not return to my apartment.  I remember the field across the parking lot used to be filled with fireflies.  There was a memorable moment, when I had been upset about something, and I was feeling depressed as usual, where I didn’t realize night had fallen.  I looked up after crying and saw a thousand glowing dots in the field.  It felt poetic to see all the fireflies, and it was quite a pretty sight.  I was feeling gloomy about my crush not liking me back.  I felt rather lonely and vented a little to my crush, and I told him “I want to sit in silence, late at night, with a super, dark clear sky and have someone with me to watch the stars” and he told me “we could do that”.  That was such a sweet, romantic thing to say.  
  
I replied, “Thought you weren’t into stargazing bro” and he said “I’m not, I’m into making good memories”.  That line definitely didn’t make me tear up and cry.  He followed it up with “One of my all time dreams has always been sitting very close with someone near a lake, at night, listening to good music, just, I don’t know, drinking tea or doing nothing.”  Well guess, what?  Half a year and two months later, we did end up dating and are still dating.  

Then a little under two years, we’ve come close to achieving that dream.  I had visited him and his family for Christmas.  One of the nights, we sat in his yard, listening to random channels being broadcast on his radio.  We found some Argentine preacher preaching away, to which I was somewhat surprised to hear.  I hadn’t thought of preachers giving sermons late at night.  It was midnight or around that time.  No fireflies though.
