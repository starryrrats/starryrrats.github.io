title: The Man
layout: post
date:  2026-06-16 22:00:00 -0500
tags: [microfiction, "tales of delusion"]
---

Beside my bed is a window.  Every night, the window lets the orange streetlight fill my room.  I can't smell the grass in this demo of an urban hellscape, so the smells of car exhaust suffice.  And some nights, there'll be a passing car or a skateboarder.  Maybe a motorcycle or a trike.  And one of these nights, I wouldn't  be surprised if a man stands on the ground, looking directly into my window and shoots a gun.  If he's smart, and he is, then he'll wait until I perform my nightly check of the outside world in its sleeping glory.  And despite this danger, I continue to look out my window every night, like the fool that I am.
